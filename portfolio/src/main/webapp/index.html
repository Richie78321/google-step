Some of my favorite projects

If you look at my GitHub you'll very likely find a jumbled mess, so here is a curated list of some of the projects I am most fond of... in no particular order.

Protip: highlight over the images to see them in motion!
Genetic Fish Tank

A virtual fish tank with virtual fish that evolve to survive in real time. They learn to search for food and avoid predators!

Impulse Physics Engine

A 2D physics engine that can simulate the interactions of arbitrary convex polygons. Includes friction, bouncing, and fracturing!

Space Farmer

One of the first games I ever made, and one of the greatest learning experiences. You're a farmer attempting to colonize an asteroid while fighting off waves of alien invaders!

Craft War

A 2D "Minecraft Clone" that features a day-night system, crafting mechanics, and caves!

Fighter Game

A 2D multiplayer arena-fighting game. It's integrated with an early version of my physics engine, and it features network-based multiplayer and a server matchmaking system!

Maze Ant

A little ant guy that uses his raytracing "antennae" to traverse random mazes! He learned to do this with my modular genetics library!
